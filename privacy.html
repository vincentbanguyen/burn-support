Support Privacy Policy

Overview

Burn is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Storage

All data — including your tasks, screen time balance, flame preferences, and app blocking settings — is stored locally on your device within an app group container. This shared container allows the main app, the device activity extension, and widgets to stay in sync. Nothing is sent to external servers.

No Analytics

We do not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks. There are no third-party SDKs in the app.

Screen Time & App Blocking

Burn uses Apple's FamilyControls, ManagedSettings, and DeviceActivity APIs to monitor usage and block apps on your device. All data is managed locally through Apple's frameworks. We never have access to your app usage data or Screen Time information.

Widgets

Burn offers home screen and lock screen widgets that display your flame status and tasks. These widgets read data from the same local app group container used by the main app. No data leaves your device.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, they are scheduled locally on your device using iOS notification services. No notification data is sent externally.

App Store Review Prompt

After using Burn for a while, iOS may show a system dialog asking you to rate the app. This is Apple's standard StoreKit review prompt. No data is collected or transmitted through this dialog.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
